Christmas Markets

Dive into the heart of Parisian holiday spirit with over a dozen festive markets offering artisanal crafts, culinary delights, and seasonal magic. Each market has unique vibes and specialties — start visiting from late November when they open, and catch the peak illuminations in the evenings.

Iconic

La Magie de Noël at Tuileries

Adjacent to the Louvre, this market features over 100 chalets with crafts, foods, and a giant Ferris wheel for panoramic views of the city.

  • Regional specialties like Alsatian pretzels and raclette
  • Skating rink and live entertainment
  • Ideal time: Late Nov to early Jan, evenings 5-10pm for magic hour

Artistic

Montmartre Market

Bohemian charm at the foot of Sacré-Cœur, featuring painter stalls, handmade jewelry, and street performers under twinkling lights.

  • Live buskers and carolers
  • Hot mulled wine and crepes
  • Ideal time: Mid-Dec to early Jan, weekends 10am-8pm

Luxurious

Champs-Élysées Market

High-end shopping experience with designer ornaments, gourmet chocolates, and luxury crafts along the iconic avenue.

  • Proximity to Arc de Triomphe
  • Elegant decorations and lights
  • Ideal time: Late Nov to mid-Dec, daily 11am-9pm

Cozy

Bastille Market

Community-focused market with eco-friendly gifts, organic wines, cheeses, and holiday crafts in a vibrant square.

  • After-market bars nearby
  • Focus on sustainable products
  • Ideal time: Early Dec to New Year's, evenings 6-11pm

Landmarks in Festive Glow

Paris's iconic sites transform into winter wonderlands with elaborate light displays, projections, and events. Plan visits for early mornings to beat crowds or evenings for the glow — the magic peaks from mid-November to early January.

Iconic

Eiffel Tower & Surrounds

The tower's hourly sparkle (5-11pm) is mesmerizing, with nearby Champs-Élysées lights and pop-up rinks.

  • Best viewed from Trocadéro
  • Seasonal projections on the tower
  • Ideal time: Dusk to midnight, especially Dec evenings
